Tavern functions as follows:
    Pulls random minions from pool of players tier or below

    Pulls 3 + players tavern tier up to 7? minions? and 1 spell unless special refreshes happen i.e. minion to have one extra spell on refresh, demon to refresh with an extra spell that costs health

    Players have the following choices in the tavern:
        Buy a card/spell

        Roll

        Freeze

        Upgrade Tavern

    There are some special cases with rolling, for example free rolls, rolls with higher tavern minions i.e. Toki
